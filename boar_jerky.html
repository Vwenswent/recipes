Boar Jerky Recipe

What are the initial steps?

First you will need too...

  1. Find Boars. Usually they will be in the meadows
  2. Kill Boars. Pick up their drops, usually hide or meat
  3. Craft a campfire and cook their meat.
  4. Apply honey to the boar meat and you will get boar jerky!

A very delicious and fulfilling meat that doesn't take very long to make. You should try it out sometime!

Tips

  • Boars can be shot with bows and arrows
  • They will run directly away from you, so you can chase them into pens
  • Never eat the raw versions of the meat
